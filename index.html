Comfort zone

The level of comfort often determines the standard of living. Therefore, we have thought of every detail. A journey into the world of impeccable taste begins with the lobby: the walls are decorated with natural stone, mirrored decorative panels, made according to the author's design.

An open eco-terrace in the eastern part of the house, quiet and protected from city noise, for those who prefer to drink a cup of coffee at dawn in the summer under the first rays of the sun, or those who decide to soak up the sun by noon. Your evening will fly by with cozy conversations in the fresh air, and in winter the terrace will turn into a private skating rink for residents and their guests.

The House has its own fitness area, on the ground floor, for the services of residents and their guests: a cafe and a grocery store, a pharmacy, dry cleaning, a beauty salon and a medical center.

In the northern part of the house, for small residents, there is a well-equipped playground for entertainment.
